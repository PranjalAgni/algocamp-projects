# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
All employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval and role requirements.

## Equipment
TechCorp provides a laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse for remote work. Employees are responsible for maintaining a suitable home office environment.

## Work Hours
Remote employees must maintain core hours of 10 AM - 3 PM in their local timezone and be available on Slack during working hours.

## Communication
Remote workers are expected to attend all team meetings via video conference and respond to messages within 2 hours during work hours.

## Security
All remote work must be performed on company-issued devices. Employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems and never share credentials.
