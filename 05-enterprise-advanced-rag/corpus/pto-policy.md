# PTO Policy

## Overview
TechCorp provides paid time off (PTO) to all full-time employees for vacation, personal time, and sick leave.

## Accrual
- Employees accrue 15 days of PTO per year (prorated for partial years)
- PTO accrues monthly at a rate of 1.25 days per month
- Maximum PTO balance cap is 30 days

## Requesting Time Off
Employees must submit PTO requests through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for approval by their manager. Emergency sick leave can be requested with shorter notice.

## Approval Process
Managers will review PTO requests within 48 hours. Requests are generally approved unless there are critical business needs or staffing conflicts.

## Carryover
Up to 10 days of unused PTO may be carried over to the next calendar year. Any PTO exceeding 10 days will be forfeited on December 31st.
