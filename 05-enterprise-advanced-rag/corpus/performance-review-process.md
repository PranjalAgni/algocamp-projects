# Performance Review Process

## Review Cycle
TechCorp conducts formal performance reviews twice per year, in June and December. Employees also have continuous feedback conversations with their managers.

## Self-Assessment
Two weeks before your review, complete a self-assessment covering:
- Key accomplishments and impact
- Progress on goals from the previous review
- Areas for growth and development
- Career aspirations

## Manager Review
Your manager will evaluate your performance against your goals and competencies for your level. They will provide written feedback and a performance rating.

## Calibration
Managers participate in calibration sessions to ensure fair and consistent ratings across the organization.

## Review Meeting
You will meet with your manager to discuss your review, feedback, and goals for the next period. This is a two-way conversation.

## Ratings
- Exceptional: Consistently exceeds expectations
- Strong: Meets and sometimes exceeds expectations
- Solid: Meets expectations
- Developing: Not yet meeting expectations; improvement plan required

## Compensation
Performance reviews inform compensation decisions including salary adjustments, bonuses, and equity grants.
