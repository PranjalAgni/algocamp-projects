# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
TechCorp is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors.

## Expected Behavior
- Treat everyone with respect and professionalism
- Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- Focus on constructive feedback and collaboration
- Assume good intent in communications

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Unwelcome physical contact or sexual attention
- Deliberate disruption of work or meetings
- Retaliation against those who report concerns

## Reporting
Violations should be reported to HR at conduct@techcorp.example or anonymously through the ethics hotline at 1-800-ETHICS. All reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially.

## Consequences
Violations may result in corrective action up to and including termination of employment.
