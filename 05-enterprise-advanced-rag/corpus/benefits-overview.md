# Benefits Overview

## Health Insurance
TechCorp offers comprehensive health insurance including medical, dental, and vision coverage. The company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 50% for dependents.

## Retirement
Employees can contribute to a 401(k) plan with employer matching up to 6% of salary.

## Time Off
- 15 days PTO annually
- 10 paid holidays
- Parental leave: 12 weeks paid
- Bereavement leave: 5 days paid

## Professional Development
$2,000 annual budget for conferences, courses, and certifications.

## Wellness
Free gym membership, mental health counseling through Employee Assistance Program, and monthly wellness stipend of $100.

## Other Perks
- Commuter benefits
- Free lunch on Wednesdays
- Annual team offsite
- Stock options (for eligible roles)
