# Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to TechCorp!
We're excited to have you join our team. This guide will help you get started in your first week.

## First Day
- Arrive at 9 AM and check in at reception
- Meet with your manager and HR for orientation
- Receive your laptop, badge, and equipment
- Set up your email, Slack, and other accounts
- Review the employee handbook and sign acknowledgements

## First Week
- Complete all required trainings (security, harassment prevention, code of conduct)
- Schedule 1:1 meetings with your team members
- Set up your development environment (Engineering only)
- Review your first project assignments
- Join relevant Slack channels and mailing lists

## 30-60-90 Day Goals
Your manager will work with you to set goals for your first 90 days. Typical milestones include:
- 30 days: Complete onboarding, understand team processes
- 60 days: Contribute to a small project independently
- 90 days: Own a significant feature or initiative
