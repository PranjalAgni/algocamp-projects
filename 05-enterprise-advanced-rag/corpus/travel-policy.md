# Travel Policy

## Booking Travel
All business travel must be approved by your manager before booking. Use the corporate travel portal (Concur) for all bookings to receive corporate rates.

## Air Travel
- Book economy class for flights under 5 hours
- Business class allowed for international flights over 5 hours
- Book at least 2 weeks in advance when possible
- Use TechCorp's preferred airlines to maximize loyalty benefits

## Accommodation
- Book reasonably priced hotels near your business destination
- Extended stay properties are preferred for trips over 5 days
- Personal stays can be added to business trips; employee covers incremental costs

## Ground Transportation
- Use public transit, rideshare, or rental cars as appropriate
- Rental cars must be compact or mid-size vehicles
- Keep fuel receipts for rental cars

## Meals and Incidentals
See the Expense Reimbursement Policy for per-diem rates and reimbursement guidelines.

## Travel Safety
Enroll in the travel registry before international trips. Review the travel safety guidelines and register your trip with the State Department if traveling to high-risk regions.
