# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Eligible Expenses
Employees may be reimbursed for reasonable business expenses including:
- Travel (flights, hotels, ground transportation)
- Meals during business travel
- Client entertainment
- Office supplies and equipment
- Professional development (with prior approval)

## Submission Process
Submit expense reports within 30 days of incurring the expense through the Expensify portal. Include itemized receipts for all expenses over $25.

## Approval
Manager approval is required for all expense reports. Finance will process approved reports within 5 business days.

## Limits
- Meals: $75/day for domestic travel, $100/day for international
- Hotels: Reasonable rates in the city of travel
- Car rentals: Compact or mid-size vehicles only

## Non-Reimbursable
Personal expenses, alcohol (except client entertainment), traffic violations, and personal car mileage over 100 miles per month.
